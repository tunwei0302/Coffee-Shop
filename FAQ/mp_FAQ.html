    • Frequently Asked Questions

    Where are you located?
    We are located at 21 Panda Street, on the Southwest corner on the same intersection as the Royal Oak Music Theater
    What is your working hours?
    We are opening from 9 a.m. to 9 p.m. on weekdays, rest between 12 p.m. to 1:30 p.m. and 6 p.m. to 7 p.m.. On Saturday, we are opening from 9 a.m. to 6 p.m., and rest on 12 p.m. to 1:30 p.m. . We are closing on Sunday.
    What kind of coffee do you serve?
    We serve Americano, Cappuccino, Espresso, Latte, Macchiato, Mocha.
    Do you have free Wi-Fi?
    Yes, we have Wi-Fi and it is free. Members just get preferred access (it’s faster)
    What kind of food do you serve?
    We serve Chocolate Whipped Cream Cake, Matcha Cream Roll, Tiramisu Cake, Matcha Tiramisu Cake.
    Do you offer barista training courses?
    No, we are only selling coffee and side dishes.
    Can I return my order?
    Our coffeehouses doesn't accept return once payment was made.
    What makes your coffee “specialty coffee”?
    Victory's Coffeehouse defines specialty coffee as being free from primary defects. It is properly sized, dried, and it presents in a cup free of faults and taints. It also has distinctive attributes, meaning it must be able to pass aspect grading and cupping tests.
